Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your Presh account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to preshapp@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address or phone number associated with your Presh account
  4. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, all user data associated with your account will be permanently removed from our systems, including:

  1. Your account profile information
  2. Your phone number and email address
  3. Any content or data you have created within the app
  4. Usage history and preferences

Data Retention

Upon processing your deletion request, all of your data will be permanently deleted. No user data will be retained after account deletion is complete.

Contact

If you have any questions about the account deletion process, please contact us at preshapp@gmail.com.
